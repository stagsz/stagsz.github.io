# Building bridges between factories and AI
About

The Story

I started my career on the production floor of Stora Enso, one of the world's largest paper companies. For 14 years, I learned every aspect of industrial manufacturing — from paper machines to Lean processes.

"Most AI developers don't understand factories. Most factory engineers don't understand AI. I speak both languages fluently — and I'm building the bridges between them."

Today, I combine deep manufacturing expertise with hands-on AI development. I build MCP servers, create digital twin pipelines, and develop tools that make industrial data accessible through natural language.

My open-source work includes servers that reduce Claude's context by 98.7%, F1 race strategy simulators processing billions of permutations, and pipelines that let you query 3D point clouds in plain English.
